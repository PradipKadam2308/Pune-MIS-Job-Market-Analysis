# 📖 Project Overview

The **MIS Companies in Pune Dashboard** is a research and data visualization project designed to provide insights into the Management Information Systems (MIS) job market across Pune.

This dashboard analyzes hiring trends, salary ranges, company distribution, experience requirements, industry segmentation, and recruitment channels, helping job seekers, students, and professionals understand the current MIS employment landscape.

The project transforms raw company data into meaningful business insights using Microsoft Excel's advanced analytics and visualization capabilities.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze the MIS job market in Pune.
- Identify top hiring companies.
- Understand salary trends based on experience.
- Study company distribution across different locations.
- Analyze hiring patterns by month.
- Compare company types and industries.
- Help freshers and professionals make informed career decisions.
